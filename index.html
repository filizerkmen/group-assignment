About Us

Espresso Lane started from a passion for coffee and to create a space of belonging. Dedicated to sustainability and quality ingredients, we offer an assortment of drinks, freshly-baked pasteries, and a space for community. We proudly use fair-trade coffee and regularly partner with local small businesses. We put time and care into our craft, and and proudly serve our coffee with top-notch latte art.
